Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08/01/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Noodle Minds we collects, uses, and protects user data when you use our Telegram Mini App.

1. Information We Collect

When you launch the App via Telegram, we receive the following limited information provided by Telegram:

  • Telegram User ID
  • First and Last Name
  • Telegram Username (if available)
  • Profile Photo (if available)

We do not have access to your phone number or your Telegram messages.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Identify you within the App
  • Display your name or profile picture

We do not share your personal data with any third parties, except as required by law.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on protected servers and used only for the purposes described in this policy. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Telegram Mini Apps do not use cookies. However, we may collect technical interaction data (such as usage timestamps or actions) to improve the user experience and application stability.
